# tri-boost — Contrarian / Risk-Auditor Brainstorm

## 1. Thesis / the bet

The dual aim is **not a free lunch — it is a frontier**, and the most dangerous failure mode is pretending otherwise. My bet: for *insurance rating* specifically, the depth-3/order-3 cap costs you almost nothing in deployable predictiveness, because pricing signal is overwhelmingly main-effect + low-order interaction, regulators *forbid* the high-order interactions black-boxes find, and what little 4-way+ signal exists is usually noise, leakage, or a proxy you'd be sued for using. So I'd build tri-boost to *win on the pricing frontier* and **instrument it to prove where it sits on the accuracy curve** rather than hide it. The whole architecture should be organized around one discipline: every component is either *inside the exact-table contract* (default audited path) or *outside it and loudly labelled* (opt-in). The biggest risk isn't the order-3 ceiling — it's **silent exactness erosion** (a calibration warp, a linear leaf, an ordered-TS axis) creeping onto the default path and quietly turning "the tables ARE the model" into "the tables approximately resemble the model." Guard that boundary obsessively and the rest is engineering.

## 2. Components

### A. The exactness firewall (the meta-component) — [EXPLAINABILITY]
A single typed boundary in the codebase: a model is either in `Exact` mode (every transform provably folds into ≤3rd-order tables, losslessness test passes bit-for-bit) or `Approximate` mode (flagged, with a measured reconstruction error reported). **Mechanism:** the losslessness invariant from research/03 §5 (per-cell reconstruction, mass conservation, per-slice zero-mean, variance-sum) runs as a *build-time assertion*, not a test you can skip. Anything that breaks it flips the mode flag and refuses to export "rating tables" — it exports "rating tables + residual model." **Tradeoff:** none for explainability; it's pure discipline. The cost is engineering friction — every feature must declare which side it's on. That friction *is* the product's integrity.

### B. Depth-3 oblivious core + Newton split + monotone/interaction constraints — [BOTH]
The non-negotiable spine: CatBoost-style oblivious growth (I1), XGBoost Newton gain + exact leaf weights `w*=−G/(H+λ)`, monotone constraints (cheap on oblivious — one constraint per level, research/04 §7), L2/min-hessian leaf regularization. **Mechanism:** oblivious → exact 3rd-order fANOVA; Newton math → accuracy parity; constraints → economic sanity + readable tables. **Tradeoff:** oblivious trees are individually weaker (AIM "honest risks"), so you pay in tree count / lower LR — must be *measured*, not assumed.

### C. Order-ceiling diagnostic + explicit residual escape hatch — [EXPLAINABILITY, hedges PREDICTIVENESS]
This is the lens-defining component. **Mechanism:** after fitting the exact order-3 model `F₃`, fit a *deliberately unconstrained* reference GBM `F_∞` (deep, leaf-wise) on the same data. Report the **gap `Dev(F_∞) − Dev(F₃)` on held-out deviance, with bootstrap CIs and calibration bands** (research/08, Calibration Bands for EDF). That number *is* the price of the order-3 cap, made visible per-portfolio. If the gap is material, offer one labelled escape hatch: an **`order-4+ residual head`** — a small, separately-declared model fit on `F_∞ − F₃` residuals, shipped *outside* the table contract as an explicit "unexplained residual" term with its own variance share. **Tradeoff:** the residual head reintroduces a black-box component — but a *quantified, bounded, optional* one, which is infinitely better than a model that silently can't fit and a user who never knows. Most pricing deployments will set residual weight to zero; the diagnostic alone is the deliverable.

### D. Interaction screening to tame C(n,3) — [BOTH]
The combinatorial explosion (research/03 §6: n=100 → 161,700 triples) is a real failure mode if the booster is left to roam. **Mechanism — three layers, all exactness-preserving:** (1) **heredity/strong-hierarchy** (admit a triple only if its sub-pairs are admitted — research/07; avoids the `O(b³)` triple-FAST scan); (2) **FAST-style RSS pre-filter as a *soft* admission prior** feeding interaction_constraints, *never* a hard gate (research/07 caveat b: FAST's objective ≠ Newton gain, so it can amputate real interactions); (3) **post-hoc exact Sobol/Shapley-effects variance pruning** — drop tables explaining negligible variance *after* purification, since pre-purification importances are unreliable (research/03 §1.5). **Tradeoff:** aggressive screening can miss a genuine triple; that's why screening is a *prior on the constraint set*, with final say going to exact post-train variance. Realized supports stay in the low hundreds, never C(n,3).

### E. Purification with reference-measure as a first-class, recomputable choice — [EXPLAINABILITY]
**Mechanism:** Lengerich mass-moving (research/03 §2), default Laplace-smoothed empirical product-of-marginals, but expose `w ∈ {uniform, empirical-product, joint/Hooker}` as a *post-hoc* flag — recompute tables under a different `w` without retraining. **The risk this hedges:** purification *ambiguity under correlated rating factors*. Correlated territory/vehicle/age factors mean the product measure evaluates effects at combinations that never occur (Hooker extrapolation), so a "pure" relativity can be an artifact of empty cells. The joint/Hooker measure fixes faithfulness but the open problem (research/07) is **estimating the 3D joint density on a sparse merged grid** — many empty cells, needs smoothing/copula factorization. **Tradeoff:** "the choice of `w` can change purified effects dramatically" — so every exported table must stamp its `w`, and importances under the joint measure must use Shapley-effects (efficiency-preserving), not raw Sobol ratios that no longer sum to 1.

### F. Per-cell credibility / stability bands — [BOTH]
Thin rating cells are where this whole thing breaks in production. **Mechanism:** inner/outer bagging of oblivious ensembles (research/07), projected onto a common merged-threshold grid, giving per-cell standard-error bands as *annotations* (computed after per-bag purification, not as fANOVA components). Plus `min_sum_hessian_in_leaf` as the exposure/credibility floor. **Tradeoff:** bagging multiplies train cost and densifies the grid (bags disagree on cuts); but it's the only honest way to show an actuary "this 3-way cell is one claim wide, do not trust it."

### G. Calibration on the right side of the firewall — [EXPLAINABILITY + calibration]
**Mechanism:** default-on **global intercept re-anchoring** (`Σμ̂=Σy`, folds into `f₀`, exactness-safe — research/07). Everything richer (local autocalibration, multicalibration) is a **separately-declared deployment-time warp**, never folded into tables. **Tradeoff:** deviance-trained GBMs lose GLM balance, so you *need* recalibration — but local autocalibration is a nonlinear warp of the aggregate score and breaks additivity. Ship it as `autocalibrate=True` post-step with a loud caveat; keep exact tables for `π̂`.

## 3. Highest-leverage ideas from my lens

1. **Ship the order-3 price tag, not just the order-3 model.** Component C's `Dev(F_∞)−Dev(F₃)` diagnostic with calibration bands is the single most de-risking thing in the project. It converts the AIM's "honest risk" into a per-deployment *measurement*. If the gap is consistently tiny on pricing data, that's your strongest sales argument; if it's large somewhere, you found out before the regulator did.

2. **Make exactness a type, not a virtue.** Component A's `Exact`/`Approximate` mode flag, enforced by build-time losslessness assertions, prevents the death-by-a-thousand-cuts failure where ordered-TS, linear leaves, and a calibration spline each individually "seemed fine" and collectively made the tables a lie.

3. **Treat the reference measure as the real research risk, not the algorithm.** Purification is solved; *which `w`* under correlated factors is not. Default product+Laplace for speed/auditability, but build the joint-measure path early and benchmark how much relativities move between measures — instability there is a credibility bomb under a regulator's questioning.

4. **Heredity-first, FAST-as-prior, variance-as-judge** for interaction selection — never let FAST hard-gate. This is the de-risked version of the screening pipeline that avoids both C(n,3) explosion and the GAMI-Tree mis-convergence trap (joint boost on realized supports + single purification pass, not EBM-style staging).

5. **A GA2M-style additive-first hybrid as the conservative default mode.** Offer `max_interaction_order ∈ {1,2,3}`. Order-1 = a pure GAM (gold-standard intelligibility, regulatory safe harbor); order-2 = matches EBM; order-3 = the differentiator. Start each fit additive, admit pairs, then triples by heredity. This makes tri-boost *strictly dominate* EBM on its own turf and gives nervous insurers a dial they understand.

## 4. What I would explicitly NOT do

- **No linear/piecewise-linear leaves on the default path.** They turn rating *tables* into rating *functions* (research/06 "changes table form"). A real accuracy lift, but it destroys the "the cell IS the relativity" property that is the entire pricing pitch. Offer only as a loudly-labelled `Approximate` opt-in mode for non-regulated use; never default.
- **No deeper trees, no leaf-wise/lossguide growth, no gblinear.** These break I1 outright (research/06 incompatible set). The temptation will be strong when chasing the last 0.5% of accuracy — resist it; that's what component C's residual head is *for*.
- **No ordered Target Statistics on the default audited path.** It's CatBoost's biggest categorical edge, but it makes a categorical axis a *learned encoding*, not the raw level — the table row no longer reads as "young drivers → 1.4×." Allowed only with explicit "encoded axis" labelling. Prefer one-hot / counter-CTR / cross-fitted encoding (exactness-preserving) for v1.
- **No soft/neural oblivious trees (NODE, NODE-GAM, GRANDE, TEL).** "Oblivious" there is a false friend — they're differentiable and break exactness. They validate the thesis; they are not the model.
- **No post-hoc table editing presented as the model** (EBM's `monotonize()`, ALE artifacts, local autocalibration folded into cells). Any warp that isn't bit-exact lives *outside* the table export, tracked separately. The number you deploy and the number you audit must be the same number — or you've lost the only thing that makes this project worth building.
